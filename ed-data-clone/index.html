The Educated Way to Purchase

How often does an opportunity come along that reduces cost, creates vast administrative savings, reduces the overall workload, and facilitates compliance with State regulations?

Educational Data Services is a turnkey Cooperative Procurement Management Program, where schools and other public entities access bid pricing to procure essential supplies & services through our one-of-a-kind e-marketplace. When you and your staff are asked to do more with less, our services allow administrators and staff to dedicate their time where it’s best served…educating children and servicing taxpayers.

Ed-Data simplifies the entire buying process, from teacher requests to PO. We partner with local government entities to organize a LOCAL cost-effective purchasing cooperative based in your state. Ed-Data serves over 700 members. Our services, originally focused on schools, have been expanded to serve other public entities such as municipalities, universities, and more.
